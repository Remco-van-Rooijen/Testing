About Me

Get to know me and my journey as a SAFe consultant

I am a passionate SAFe 6.0 Implementation Consultant with a deep understanding of Agile methodologies and the Scaled Agile Framework. My mission is to help organizations achieve business agility by implementing SAFe principles and practices.

With a background in software development, project management, and organizational transformation, I bring a unique perspective to Agile coaching. I have worked with diverse teams across various industries, from startups to Fortune 500 companies, helping them scale Agile practices and deliver value more effectively.

My approach is collaborative and pragmatic. I believe in tailoring SAFe implementations to fit the specific needs and culture of each organization, ensuring sustainable and impactful change.
